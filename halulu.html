Ingredients

  • Crushed or shaved ice
  • Sweetened saba bananas, jackfruit (langka), macapuno / nata de coco
  • Sweetened beans or sweetened red beans / garbanzos / munggo
  • Gulaman or jelly cubes (optional)
  • Iced milk or evaporated / chilled milk
  • Ice cream (ube, vanilla, or flavor of choice)
  • Condensed milk or sugar syrup (optional)

Instructions

  • Layer sweetened fruits, beans, jelly, macapuno at the bottom of a tall glass.
  • Fill the glass with crushed or shaved ice almost to the top.
  • Pour milk over the ice until it covers some of the layers.
  • Add a scoop (or two) of ice cream on top.
  • Drizzle condensed milk or syrup if you want extra sweetness.
